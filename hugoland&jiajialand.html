About Hugoland & JiaJialand

Hugoland and JiaJialand are unique, dynamic nations with a rich history, diverse culture, and innovative spirit. Together, they form a powerful alliance that combines the best of both worlds.

History

Hugoland was founded in 1846 and recognized in 1913. It fought a great war in 2013, expanding its territory significantly. In 2024, it joined forces with JiaJialand to share its inventions, leading to a new era of collaboration and growth.

Sports

The Hugolympics are a highlight in Hugoland, featuring unique sports such as pencil throw, eraser put, and rulering. The games are held annually in Pershilia, Salavila, and Magel, attracting top athletes from around the world.

Business

Hugoland and JiaJialand are known for their innovative technologies, including the hoverboard, Level 8 A.I, worldwide emails, and the flying car. These inventions have positioned them as leaders in the global market.
